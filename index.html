Features

Pi-Fi is packed with powerful features to monitor your children and keep them earning time online

Set Online Time and Limits

You control When and How Long

Set absolute limits, like no more than an hour per day, or no access after 9 p.m., to reflect how your want to parent your children.

Dashboard

Monitor Usage and Activities

Pi-Fi gives parents a dashboard to view online time, time per device, completed activities, and activity performance at a glance.

Customized Activities

Academics, Creative, or Chores

You can chose activities that reinforce age-appropriate academic skills, such as math, science, and vocabulary, to those that induce creativity, like journal and novel writing, or music, to those that enforce religious values.

All Devices, One System

Laptops, Computers, iPad, Xbox

Pi-Fi does the hard work and controls time across of your child's web-enabled devices. Your child can earn time on her iPad, move to the Wii, and run out of time on the family laptop. It's all seamless to make it easy.
