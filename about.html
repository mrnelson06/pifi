About Us

Pi-Fi is a pre-seed startup business based out of Albuquerque, NM. Started at Startup Weekend Albuquerque in April 2014, we're developing both a hardware and software cloud platform that makes it easy for parents to manage when, and for how long, their children are online across all a child's devices, wherever their children may be. Children can earn more Internet screen time by solving activities, ranging from those that reinforce academics, such as math, vocabulary, and science problems, to those that induce creativity, like journal writing, to those that imbue religious values, like Bible study.
